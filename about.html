About Us

We are the wolrd's largest university

Established in 2001, our program has quickly become a respected and dynamic force in the region. Initially created to meet the growing demand for skilled IT professionals, we started with a modest cohort of twenty students. Over the years, we have expanded our reach globally. Our journey began in a small yet well-equipped facility with two dedicated instructors who were passionate about technology and education. Their vision was clear: to develop a curriculum that blends technical skills with real-world applications. The early success of our graduates, who went on to lead major tech initiatives, laid the foundation for our reputation. In 2010, we reached a milestone with the expansion of our campus facilities. This included the addition of advanced labs simulating real-world tech environments and the inauguration of our main library, now housing over 10,000 specialized resources in technology and applied sciences. Recognized with numerous awards and accreditations, we are committed to maintaining excellence in technology education. Our mission remains steadfast: to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and experience to thrive in the ever-evolving tech sector. Join us in contributing to the rich history of innovation and success in technology education. Discover how you can shape the future of technology with us.
